# Deep Workflow

The deep workflow follows the standard research shape with a higher research budget:

1. Plan the research.
2. Run official, recent, and broad research lanes in parallel.
3. Review coverage and conflicts.
4. Run up to two rounds of gap research when critical gaps remain.
5. Produce the final answer and structured output.

Runtime orchestration lives in `workflow.py`. Deep-specific settings live in `config.py`.
